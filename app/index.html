Kayak Bar is located in the heart of openhagen, in the middle of Børsen, Danmarks Nationalbank and Christiansborg, and right down to the canals. The whole area is protected and the historical surroundings give the place a very special touch - a personality to be experienced! Our premises are located right down to the water - so close that we have our own floating beach. We are a cafe, a lunch place, a bar, a focal point for kayakers and a cultural venue - and a lot more. We hold a wide range of events: concerts, events, exhibitions, courses, catwalks, plate releases, corporate events, festivals, weddings and other parties.
